Privacy Policy

Nexora respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

We may collect your name, email, phone number, business information, and any information you submit through our forms. We may also collect technical data such as IP address, browser type, device information, and interaction data through cookies and analytics tools.

How We Use Information

We use your information to respond to inquiries, provide our services, improve our website, and send service-related SMS messages if you opt in.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect the personal information we collect. These measures include secure server environments, encrypted data transmission (HTTPS), access controls, and routine monitoring for unauthorized access. While we take steps to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

SMS Communications

Nexora may send SMS messages to users who voluntarily opt in through our online forms or other consent-based methods. By providing your phone number and opting in, you consent to receive SMS messages related to customer support, service updates, appointment reminders, and optional promotional content if selected. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP, or request assistance by replying HELP.

Nexora does not share, sell, or transfer SMS opt-in data to any third parties. SMS consent is used exclusively for messaging purposes directly related to Nexora’s services.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Nexora may use cookies, analytics tools, and similar tracking technologies to improve website performance, understand user behavior, and enhance your experience. These tools may collect information such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and interaction patterns. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but some features of the site may not function properly without them.

User Rights

You may request access to your data, correction of inaccurate information, deletion of your data, or opt out of SMS or email communications at any time.

Mobile Information Sharing Statement

We do not share mobile numbers or SMS consent with third parties except as required to deliver messages through our SMS provider.
